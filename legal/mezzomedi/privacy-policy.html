MezzoMedi — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

MezzoMedi is an offline guide to Mediterranean flavor pairings, published by ShadowTune (Frederic Valente), based in France ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, why, and how it's handled — in short, almost nothing, and nothing personal ever leaves your device.

What we access

The pairing database. All ingredients, flavor profiles, pairings, and photos ship inside the app itself. MezzoMedi works entirely offline and makes no network requests — nothing is downloaded, looked up, or sent while you use it.

Favorites and app settings. If you save favorite ingredients or pairings, or change app preferences, that information is stored only on your device. It never leaves your phone or tablet, and uninstalling the app deletes it.

Purchases. If you unlock the full app, the purchase is handled entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see your credit card or payment details — Google does, under Google's Privacy Policy. We only learn that "this device owns the unlock" so the rest of the app becomes available.

What never leaves your device

MezzoMedi does not send any personal information off your phone or tablet. In particular, the app does not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email, or contact information.
  • Your location.
  • Your contacts, photos, calendar, or any other device data.
  • The microphone — the app does not use it and does not request permission for it.
  • Advertising identifiers or any persistent tracking identifier.

No special permissions

MezzoMedi requests no Android permissions. It does not need internet access, location, the camera, the microphone, your contacts, or your photos to work — everything it shows is built into the app.

No ads or tracking

MezzoMedi contains no third-party advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking tools. We do not build profiles of users, and we do not sell or share any data — because nothing personal leaves the device in the first place.

Children's privacy

MezzoMedi is suitable for users of all ages. Because no personal information ever leaves your device and we show no ads, we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. There is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf — uninstalling the app removes all local data, including favorites and settings.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date above and may note significant changes within the app.
